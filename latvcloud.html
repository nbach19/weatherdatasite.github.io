Latitude vs. Cloudiness

In this analysis, it appears as though the latitude of any given location does not have a major effect on the cloud coverage. As you can see from the graph, the plot is spread out fairly evenly, with many Latitudes corresponding to a wide variety of cloudiness levels. This was to be expected, as clouds are most often shaped by wind patterns.
